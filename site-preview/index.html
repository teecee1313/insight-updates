What it does

Seven scans, plus news and a practice portfolio.

Every scan works from end-of-day price and volume — the same data the professionals watch — and ranks the whole market so the interesting names rise to the top. Nothing here is a recommendation; it's a faster way to decide what to research.

🎯

Smart Money Scan

Surfaces shares showing sustained buying — multiple up-days paired with volume well above their own average.

🚨

Sharp Mover Detector

Flags moves that are abnormal for that specific share, not just big in absolute terms.

🔍

Unusual Activity Radar

Weighs five behaviours together — volume spikes, price streaks, quiet accumulation, range breaks and momentum — into one score that pinpoints shares trading unlike their normal selves.

🔥

Accumulation

Detects quiet, persistent buying building up over days — the pattern that often precedes a move.

🏆

Top 20 to Watch

A single ranked shortlist, scored out of 10, blending every signal into one daily watchlist.

🧩

All Signals Scan

Runs everything together and ranks by how many signals each share is firing at once.

🌋

Volume Surge Report

Ranks every share by how far today's volume beats its own average — tagged 🟢 buying or 🔴 selling so a heavy-volume fall never masquerades as a bullish signal. Filter by heat level or direction.
